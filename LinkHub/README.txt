LinkHub (Pro) — by Jerson
---------------------------------
Files:
- index.html
- css/style.css
- js/main.js
- assets/cover.jpg  (replace with your album artwork)
- assets/preview.mp3 (optional 30–60s preview)

How to use:
1) Drop this folder (e.g., /album_linkhub) in your site root.
2) Update platform link URLs in index.html.
3) Replace assets/cover.jpg and assets/preview.mp3.
4) Link from your main site cards: href="album_linkhub/index.html".
