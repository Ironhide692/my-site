About

Hello, I am Jerson Rivas! A musician, producer, and sound designer based in Los Angeles. I recently graduated from UC San Diego with a B.A. in Interdisciplinary Computing in the Arts (Music) and a minor in Computer Science. My process fuses real-world recordings with electronic and acoustic elements to craft experiences that are technical, tactile, and emotionally driven— not only to entertain, but also to provoke thought and reflection.

Beyond electronic composition, I am also deeply rooted in live performance. My versatility allows me to move effortlessly between digital production and traditional instrumentation, bringing the same creative energy to both. My drumming has become a defining part of my musical identity, earning recognition by the UC San Diego Musicians Club as Best Drummer (2024, 2025).
